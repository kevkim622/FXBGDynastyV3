Rules and Regulations

FXBG Dynasty League Constitution

Last Updated: 12/13/2022


  1. Commissioner will establish a rules committee that will check the powers of the commissioner and foresee over all rulings throughout the year.
  2. Buy-in will be at $50 per person.
    • If you wish to trade future draft picks, you must pay buy-in for the full current year plus a quarter of the year that you wish to trade.
  3. Trades can be vetoed if there is a unanimous vote from all parties not involved in the trade to veto.
    • Multi-team trades are allowed.
    • There will be a 24 hour window a trade can be vetoed by the entire league.
  4. Amendments can be made to the Constitution during the year but cannot go into effect until the following year.
    • Amendments must be approved by the entire league in a majority vote (7/10).
    • Unless otherwise deemed necessary and pushed through by commissioner and rules committee.***
  5. League ends after the Consolation Championship game.
    • Trades will not be allowed until March 1st (League reopens).
    • IR slots must be cleared before league end.
    • Free agents can not be picked up until after the league has drafted.
  6. Waivers will be decided using FAAB (Free Agent Acquisition Budget).
    Content for list item
  7. Waivers will be decided using FAAB (Free Agent Acquisition Budget).
    • Teams will have $150 to start the league year (March 1st) and will not be allotted more FAAB until the following year.
    • Players on waivers will not be able to move for 2 days.
    • Waivers reset on Tuesday but if official NFL games are pushed back, then the waivers will reset after the last game of that week.
  8. Trade deadline will be week 13.
  9. Playoffs will be held week 15 and 16.
    • One week playoff games.
  10. Our first draft will be held with vets only and a seperate draft will be held for rookies only.
    • The first veteran draft will be held over the course of a few days and each person will have 8 hours to make a pick, this is so that people will have time to go about their normal lives and possibly make a trade.
    • You are under no obligation to use the full 8 hours, the hope is for you to take the time to make a trade happen or get ready to make your next pick.
    • The rookie draft will be a live draft where teams will have 2 minutes to draft instead of the 8 hours allotted for veterans.
    • The commissioner will pause the draft at 11 pm and start the draft up again at 9 am so that people have time to sleep and get ready for work.
    • Every draft following the initial startup draft, will strictly be for rookies and free agents.
    • Both drafts will not allow for auto-draft unless after the maximum of 8 hours has been taken.
  11. In the case of possible collusion, the rules committee will determine if the person or party is guilty and will be permanently banned from the league.
    • "Collusion" happens when two or more teams in the league conspire together to affect some outcome in the league.
    • A common act of “illegal cooperation” for example, would be trading between two players to purposefully help one player gain an unfair edge against another opponent. They might work together to try to make a “super team” to ensure that one of them will win the league.
    • The rules committee will be judging according to the previous 2 guidelines.
  12. If people are deciding to leave the league, the person will give the commissioner 2 months notice and find a replacement.
    • If no replacement can be found, the commissioner will be held responsible to find a new member. If the leaving party cannot find a replacement, they will owe half the next years buy-in.
  13. Roster spots will be as follows:
    • 1 QB
    • 2 RB
    • 3 WR
    • 2 FlEX(RB/WR/TE)
    • 1 TE
    • 1 SF (QB/RB/WR/TE)
    • 16 Bench
    • 3 IR
      • IR slots will be taken up by players that are designated as OUT.
    • 5 taxi
      • Rookies can be taken off taxi but cannot be put back on.
      • You cannot replace taxi spots with new rookies or veterans.
      • Taxi slots will lock after the 4th week of preseason.
      • If you trade a player on taxi, they will be forfeiting their taxi slot unless you are trading from another team's taxi.
      • Taxi players can stay on taxi for a maximum of 2 years.
  14. After the end of the league year, teams must cut the roster down to 18.
    • You will have until after the Consolation Championship game to do this.
    • If you do not cut down your roster to the appropriate amount of players, you will lose draft picks starting with your least valuable to the most valuable every day that your roster is not cut down.
  15. Scoring will be Half PPR (Half Point Per Reception).
    • The rest is all standard scoring.
  16. Cash prizes will be rewarded as follows:
    • 1st: $300
    • 2nd: $150
    • 3rd: $50
  17. 4 teams will make the playoffs. The draft order of the bottom 6 teams will be determined by MPPF (Max Potential Points For).
    • The 4 playoff teams will draft after the bottom 6 and according to their placing in the playoffs.
  18. The winner of the consolation bracket will be rewarded with a draft pick in the end of the 4th round of rookie drafts.
  19. The bottom 2 teams from each division will swap the following year.
  20. Last place will be required to give a formal presentation on why his team failed and how he can improve the following year.
  21. Tiebreakers will go as follows: (W/L), Points For, H2H, Points Against, Away Wins, Home Wins, Bench Scoring
  22. All teams will be required to pick a city and mascot for their team name, as it keeps everything uniform.
  23. All trades that are approved by all parties involved will be subjected to a veto system made up of your peers.
    • These vetoes must total up to 8 managers.
      • All vetoed trades will be reviewed by the Rules Committee to make a final ruling.
  24. The commissioner and rules committee will make amendments only if deemed absolutely necessary throughout the year.
    • These amendments will be voted on by the entirety of the league and will have to pass with a true majority (7/10).
  25. The most important rule in this league: Don't be an idiot.
