Colton Hall

aka "The Little Engine that Could"

Team: Sugar Tit Bungos

In a league full of football diehards, we land on a manager who has the most laid back approach to his dynasty team. A man who would much rather jump into a Destiny raid or Balder's Gate rerun, Colton has still managed to earn at least one playoff berth which is more than most teams in this league can say. With the help of our Lord and Savior Joe Burrow, anything is possible. With a team full of older vets and rookies, and nothing in between, he continues to compete on a weekly basis and prove a lot of doubters wrong year after year.
