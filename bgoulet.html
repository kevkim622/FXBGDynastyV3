Brian Goulet

aka "The Over-Thinker"

Team: Frederick Families

Even with the hope of Watson becoming his old self, nobody in the league is willing to take on all the bad juju that comes along with trading for him so he's stuck. Goulet will be undertaking one of the biggest tank jobs of this league's history in order to secure the 1st overall pick in the 2024 NFL Draft. With this in mind, most managers should actually be thanking him for his donation for the next 2-3 years while his rebuild is fully underway. Thank you.
