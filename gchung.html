Gene Chung

aka "Bottom Gun"

Team: Duuval Dirty Dogs

With the 2nd lowest win percentage all-time, Gene needs to find a new way to motivate his players into getting him some W's under his belt. With the 4th most transactions in the league you would think the results would eventually turn his team around but we have yet to see anything mildly competitive. While this could be the year in which he turns it around, he will be banking on the likes of older veteran RB's that do get hurt like its their side hustle. If they do manage to stay healthy he has a legit shot at playoffs in a weaker division than he was last year. Constantly being 7th must get to a man at some point, hopefully the moves he made this past offseason can put him over the 6 win hump and into the playoffs. For now, he remains "Chipper" from Top Gun. Yeah, me neither.
